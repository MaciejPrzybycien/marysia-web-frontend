Marysia Software Limited has been created and is run by two old friends – Adam Manczuk and Maciej Przybycien. Originally the company mobile office was planned on the boat but technical issues have delayed sailing from Norway, where the boat – legendary Colin Archer - later named Marysia has been bought.
